PRIMARY STORY IDEA TO CONVEY
The Rattrap is a moral and symbolic story. The “rattrap” is not only the object the peddler sells; it is also the world itself, which lures people with bait such as money, comfort, and pleasure. The story finally shows that compassion and respect can awaken goodness even in a troubled person.

1. Extended Metaphor
“The world is a rattrap.”

2. Symbolism

Cheese / bait = material comforts
Forest = confusion and punishment
Christmas = hope and renewal
Money = lure
Gift of rattrap = redemption

3. Irony
The thief becomes morally better by the end.
The man expected to cheat others is the one who is transformed.

4. Imagery
The forest, the cottage, the forge, and the old-fashioned scenes create vivid pictures.

5. Contrast
Trust vs suspicion
Poverty vs wealth
Cruelty vs compassion
Temptation vs redemption

6. Foreshadowing
The idea of the world as a trap prepares the reader for the peddler’s later moral and physical entrapment.

7. Personification
The world is presented as if it actively lays traps for people.

8. Moral allegory
The story works like a symbolic moral lesson about human weakness and goodness.
